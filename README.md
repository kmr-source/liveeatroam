## ✨ Live • Eat • Roam

A foodie adventure through the streets of **Vancouver & beyond**.

This project is a **personal creator hub** designed to showcase short-form content including:

- 🎬 Instagram Reels
- 🎥 Video highlights
- 📸 Photo moments
- 🍜 Food discoveries
- ✈️ Travel stories
- 🌿 Daily lifestyle

---

## 🎬 Features

### Reels Showcase
Embedded Instagram reels displayed directly on the site so visitors can watch content without leaving the page.

### Video & Photo Sections
Dedicated sections to explore more visual content from travel and food experiences.

### Creator Navigation
Simple navigation for quick access:

- Reels
- Videos
- Photos
- About
- Instagram Profile
